Site Name

The Red Gold Hub (TRGH)

This name represents a hub dedicated to Nigeria's palm oil industry, highlighting its historical significance as "red gold" and its potential for economic revitalization.

Site Purpose

The Reason I chose this because palm oil is a pillar of the Nigerian economy, yet we;ve gone from being the world's top exporter in the 1960s to a net importer today. Building this site allows me to tell an important local story while using JavaScript to handle the Supply Gap data showing how a boost in local tech could help bridge our 500,000 metric ton deficit.

Scenarios

Let say a users asks a question like this "I am looking to invest in local processing mills; where can I see the current data on the national supply deficit to understand the market potential?"

Or another user asks a question like this "What happened to Nigeria's palm oil production since the 1960s, and what specific modern technologies are being proposed to return us to our status as a top exporter?"
